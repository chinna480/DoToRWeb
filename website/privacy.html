🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

📋 Quick Summary: DoToR collects only the data needed to provide doorstep repair services — your name, phone number, location, and device photos. We never sell your data. We use Firebase (Google) for secure storage and Cloudinary for image hosting. You can request deletion anytime.

1. Introduction

Welcome to DoToR ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using DoToR, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our doorstep repair service:

Data Type Purpose Required?
Name To identify you and share with your assigned technician Yes
Phone Number To contact you about your order and for the technician to reach you Yes
Device Location (GPS) To find nearby technicians and enable live tracking of your repair Yes
Service Address To send the technician to the correct location Yes
Device Photos/Videos To help technicians diagnose the issue before arriving No
Order Details To track repair history and improve our services Yes
Device Model/Brand To help technicians bring the right tools and parts No
Push Notification Token To send real-time updates about your repair No

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: When you fill out the booking form, create an account, or contact us.
  • Automatically: When you use our app, we collect your GPS location (with your permission) to enable technician tracking.
  • From your device: When you upload photos or videos, we process them through our image hosting service (Cloudinary).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect you with nearby technicians for doorstep repair
  • To provide real-time tracking of your technician's location
  • To send push notifications about order status (technician assigned, nearby, arrived, completed)
  • To enable in-app chat between you and your technician
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you about your orders and support requests

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Realtime Database to store all order data, user profiles, chat messages, and reviews. Firebase is GDPR and SOC2 compliant. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary: We use Cloudinary to store and optimize photos and videos you upload. Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Expo Push Notifications: We use Expo's push notification service to send real-time alerts to your device. Expo Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps (via Leaflet/OSM): We use OpenStreetMap with Leaflet for map displays. Google Maps deep links are used for navigation, but no map data is sent to Google by us.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Data is stored securely on Firebase servers (Google Cloud infrastructure)
  • Transmission of data is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Only authorized personnel and the assigned technician can access your order details
  • We do not store credit card or payment information

7. Data Sharing

We share your information only as necessary to provide the service:

  • With your assigned technician: Your name, phone number, address, device details, and issue description are shared with the technician assigned to your order so they can perform the repair.
  • With service providers: As described in Section 5 (Firebase, Cloudinary, Expo).
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

8. Location Data

DoToR collects your device's GPS location for the following purposes:

  • Customers: To find nearby technicians and share your repair location with the assigned technician for navigation purposes.
  • Technicians: To share your real-time location with customers for live tracking, and to find nearby job requests.

Location is only collected while the app is in use (foreground) or when you have an active order being tracked (background, with permission). You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Order history is kept to allow you to review past repairs and submit reviews. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 11).

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for location access or push notifications at any time

11. Contact & Data Deletion Requests

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights (including requesting deletion of your data), please contact us:

  • Email: support@dotor.in
  • In-App: Use the help/support option in your profile
  • Website: Visit dotor.in and use the contact form

12. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Consent

By using DoToR, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. When you book a repair, you explicitly consent to sharing your location, contact information, and device details with the assigned technician.
